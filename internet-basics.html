üí°What is the Internet?

The internet is a huge network of computers all over the world. It allows us to open websites, watch videos, send emails and chat with people. You can think of it as a giant library + post office + TV + playground combined!

‚úÖGood Uses of the Internet

  • Watching educational videos (science, maths, stories).
  • Using Google to search answers for homework.
  • Attending online classes from school.
  • Using email to send projects to teachers.

‚ö†Ô∏èBe Careful With

  • Fake news and wrong information on random websites.
  • Unknown people asking to chat or video call.
  • Websites that ask for your password or personal details.
  • Spending too much time on games instead of study.

üß≠How to Open a Website (Step-by-step)

  1. Open a browser like Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click on the address bar at the top.
  3. Type the website name (for example: www.nationalgeographic.com).
  4. Press Enter.
  5. Explore the page by scrolling up and down.

üéâFun Facts

  • There are billions of websites, but we use only a few daily.
